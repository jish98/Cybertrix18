EVENTS

Join your hands and come to our college and participate in our events

  • Blind Code

    2 per Team

    An event where participants are asked to code without the monitors switched on

    • Round 1 :Identify the language
    • Round 2:Story code
    • Round 3:Blind code

  • TECHPIC

    2 per Team

    An event where participants are given a few games in the form of puzzles and are asked to solve them.

    • Round 1:Crossword Puzzles
    • Round 2:Connexions
    • Round 3:Googler

  • THE BUG HUNTER SQUAD

    2 per Team

    the participants are asked to DEBUG a program and application.

    • Round 1:MCQ'S
    • Round 2:Program Debugging
    • Round 3:Application Debugging

  • ENCRYPT-O-CONNECT

    2 per Team

    Participants should work on encryption and decryption techniques.

    • Round 1:Basic Encryption(Find the key)
    • Round 2:Program Decryption
    • Round 3:Enc-net connect the system with encrypted key.

  • WALK-IN

    Individual

    Participants will be experiencing a mini placement where they'll be tested on TECHNICAL SKILLS,COMMUNICATION SKILLS and PROGRAMMING SKILLS

    • Round 1:Technical MCQ's
    • Round 2:Group Discussion
    • Round 3:Coding
